About me

Curious about me? Here you have it:

I'm a Computer Engineering and Artificial Intelligence student, passionate about exploring how smart systems can learn, adapt, and solve real-world problems. Technology excites me not just for its logic, but for its ability to shape a better future.

Since I began my studies, I've been fascinated by fields like machine learning, data science, and intelligent algorithms. I love experimenting with ideas, building projects, and discovering how theory can transform into practical solutions.

Every new challenge makes me more curious and motivates me to keep growing. From coding assignments to personal projects, I enjoy combining creativity and technical skills to bring ideas to life.

Looking ahead, my goal is to keep exploring the vast field of artificial intelligence, from deep learning to natural language processing and robotics in the best way.
